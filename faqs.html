Accountant

Details
Accountants are responsible for preparing and examining financial records, ensuring their accuracy, and making sure that taxes are paid properly and on time. They also provide organizations with financial insights, help in financial planning, and maintain records of financial transactions. Key Responsibilities:
  1. Financial Recording: Accountants record financial transactions, including purchases, sales, receipts, and payments, in various financial systems and software.
